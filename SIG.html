Student Interest Groups

Student Interest Groups, or SIGs, are what make the SpeedACM the SpeedACM. SIGs offer students to explore the broad field of "Computer Enginering Computer Science" to find your niche. Through SIGs, members are exposed to a number of aspects of computing that you will encounter while working that the school just doesn't have time to cover.
